This file is a **placeholder** for the future Commercial License Agreement that will govern the **Egide Professional** and **Egide Enterprise** editions.

## Why dual licensing

Egide Community is licensed under **GNU AGPL-3.0-only**. The AGPL requires that any
modified version offered as a network service must publish its modifications.

Some customers cannot or will not comply with AGPL terms, typically because:

- They want to integrate Egide as a closed-source SaaS without releasing modifications.
- They need contractual indemnification, SLAs, or warranties.
- They embed Egide in proprietary products distributed to their customers.

For these customers, Egide offers a commercial license alongside additional features
(see `docs/editions.md`). The two paths coexist; same codebase for the core, separate
modules for premium features.

## What the commercial license will provide

- Right to use Egide without AGPL copyleft obligations.
- Access to **Professional** / **Enterprise** edition modules (multi-tenant, air-gapped
  bundle, advanced policy targets, white-label, SSO/SAML, etc.).
- Software updates and security patches under the contractual term.
- Email or 24/7 support depending on tier.
- Optional indemnification.
